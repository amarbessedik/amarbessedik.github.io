I want to talk a bit about my background (you can also read my blog posts where I will dive into details about my interests in technology and software). My name is Amar Bessedik, I graduated from West Chester University of Pennsylvania (WCUPA) in May 2018. Before saying more about my experience as a graduate student in the USA, I First immigrated to Belgium in late 2008 from Algeria after graduation with a Computer Science Engineering Degree. By that I was hoping to continue my graduate studies at the Free University of Brussels (ULB) to enhance my skills in the Computer Science field. Unfortunately, things did not go as I planned with regards to funding! Then I immigrated to the USA in the summer of 2010 where I first got into a Graduate Certificate in Web Technology Program at WCUPA in Fall 2014. I aimed to get exposure to the current web technologies and programming practices. All the courses I took were graduate level courses which are transferable to the Master's program, but the more I studied for my Certificate Degree the more I felt I was'nt prepared to enter the professional world just yet. Upon successful completion of my Certificate Degree, I applied and got into to the Master's Program. I graduated and I am finally satisfied with my education.

My objective is to proffessionally master my own craft, I want to deliver the best of me in the software industry and I am aiming to be very successful. I want especially to master Database systems design, learn and apply the best software Engineering practices, learn and apply my skills using web technologies. I am also intrested in mobile development as most business is done online and people are reacheable through their handheld devices. Everyone has a story, I told mine and I would like it to be as my compass in the way to the next level of my journey.
